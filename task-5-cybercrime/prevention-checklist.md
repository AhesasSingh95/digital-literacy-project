Preventing UPI frauds:
Preventing yourself from UPI frauds requires a combination of technological tools and behavioural caution. Here are some effective strategies:
1)Enable multi-factor authentication:
Use biometric authentication or two factor verification for your added security.

2)Verify links and sender details:
Always double check URLs and sender information before clicking on any link or sharing personal details.

3)Regularly update your UPI app;
Keep your UPI app updated to benefit from the latest security features.

4)Avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions:
Public networks are vulnerable to hacking. Use a secure network internet connection for UPI payments.

5)Set transaction limits:
Limit your daily transaction amount to minimise potential losses.

6)Educate yourself and others:
Stay updated about the latest fraud tactics and educate your family.

7)Screen sharing apps:
Avoid using screen sharing apps like Any desk and team viewer.
 
8)QR code alert:
Do not send money through QR codes sent through E-Mails and WhatsApp.
